Magnet Bar (work with end block as rotary magnetron source)

Description:

1. Magnetic uniformity is continuously adjustable at multiple points with washers,which is quantifiable for record, thus more reliable.

2. Versatile and optimized simulation to enhance magnetic field design, custom to meet different coating process requirements.

3. Seamless encapsulation of stainless steel tube, the magnets are not water exposed, stable performance with a much longer service lifetime.

Specifications:
  • ID 56mm OD70mm
  • ID 80mm OD100mm
  • ID 125mm OD152mm
  • Length: up to 4000mm
  • Magnet Strength: standard, strong; bespoke per requirements: 300-1200Gs
  • Magnetic Strength Adjustable Range: 400-1200Gs
  • Power Supply Unit: DC/Pulsed-DC/MF/RF
