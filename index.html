Products :

Rotary Magnetron Sputter Source (Rotary Magnetron)

Patent end block structure with waterway 100% filled with cold water, ensure cooling of bombarding area (especially important for roll2roll web coating or sputtering upwards from bottom) thereinafter, higher process power/lower substrate thermal effect - nearly 100% water drainage during target exchange, no water leakage to vacuum sealing.

Planar Magnetron Sputter Deposition Source (Planar Magnetron)

Versatile and optimized simulation to enhance magnetic field design, custom to meet different coating process requirements.

Magnet Bar (work with end block as rotary magnetron source)

Magnetic uniformity is continuously adjustable at multiple points with washers,which is quantifiable for record, thus more reliable.

Round Magnetron Sputter Source(Circular Flat Magnetron)

Target Diameter:4, 6, 8, 10, 12 inches,
Electrode: M8 stud,7/16 connector or copper stud,
Mounting Type: Internal-mount, External-mount,
Cooling Design: Direct cooling,Indirect cooling,
Magnet strength: standard, strong.
